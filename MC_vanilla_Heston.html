Call Option by Monte Carlo Simulation with Importance Sampling Technique under the Heston Model

Input:
Initial underlying asset price
Initial volatility (annulized)
The rate of mean reversion of V
The long term mean level of V
Strike price
Time to maturity Years
Risk free interest rate (annulized)
The correlation of stock price and V
Volatility of V (annulized)
Number of partitions
Number of paths
Output:
Call value

The calculation is based on Monte Carlo method with Importance Sampling Technique.
